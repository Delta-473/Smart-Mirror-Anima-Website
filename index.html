Explaining the hardware / components :

Block scheme :

Explaining :

In this block diagram you see how the project is put together. It may change in the future because the reflection we wanted is not achieved. Momentairy we have taken a old cheap lcd screen with a vga connection. This can be changed in the future to get even bether screne quality. On the screen we have placed a plexi glass with a special foil on it this foil is responsible for the mirroring of the screen. Above that we have placed a resistive touchscreen panel this very cheap but doesnt work al that great so we advice to use a capacitive touchscreen and dubble check that it is a clear touchscreen NOT mat ;).
All of that will be connected to a rasberry pi where the sofware on runs. On the rasberry pi we have connected also 2 sesnors. The left one will be used to mesure the temperature and the right one will capute movement. This will enable the mirror and go to sleep when there isnt any movement found.

Thomas More university

Campus De Nayer

Thomas More is the largest university college in Flanders, offering over 30 Dutch-taught and 5 English-taught bachelor degree programs in the province of Antwerp. Next to that, Thomas More offers exchange programmes in English, for students from partner universities. Thomas More was created by joining together the strengths of Katholieke Hogeschool Kempen, Lessius Antwerp and Lessius Mechelen. With Thomas More (1478-1535) the university college follows in the footsteps of the English humanist, scientist and author. In addition, the name “More” also refers to more course programs, more regional spread, more cooperation agreements, more horizons.
